Chesterfield Half Marathon

20/Mar, 10am
Outdoors

Join this half marathon and help raise money for charity. The route will take you through the beautiful countryside of Derbyshire, with stunning views of the Peak District. The route is flat and fast, with a few small hills to keep you on your toes. The route is fully marshalled and there will be water stations along the way. The event is chip timed and you will receive a medal and goody bag at the finish line.

Location: Starting at Highfield Park, Chesterfield, UK

Capacity: Unlimited

Assistance (estimated): 8000

Price: Free
